Empowering women through regenerative fashion and community development

Seeds & Stories is women-led social enterprise that empowers women through fashion.

Our products and vocational training create economic opportunities and meaningful livelihoods for Ugandan women, promote regenerative community development while having a positive impact on the local environment.

Discover Bigodi, Uganda

We are based in Bigodi, a village rich in natural materials but which is suffering from the effects of environmental degradation due to human activities.
Seeds & Stories’ mission is to combat environmental degradation and contribute to Bigodi’s environmental regeneration through education and vocational training, helping the population build a circular, self-sufficient, community-based economy. We offers training on permaculture, regenerative agriculture, waste management, and water conservation. .

Discover our vocational training

Our artisans only use organic and sustainably harvested natural local fibres, including backcloth, palm leaf, millet straws, papyrus, raffia, colona maranta, and banana fibre. We only use earth-based dyes, a combination of food waste, plants, leaves, seeds, roots, barks and clay.

Regenerative fashion

sub-heading here

Seeds & Stories strives to create products that leave the world in a better place than when we started. We create unique, stylish, and timeless fashion items for women that want to make a difference. By shopping with us you can help reduce poverty and regenerate the natural environment in rural Uganda.

Regenerative fashion is important to us. We pledge to only use local raw materials that have regenerative properties in local ecosystems, namely increasing soil fertility, enhancing biodiversity and increasing the rate of carbon sequestration from the atmosphere into the soil contributing, in this way, to climate change mitigation.
