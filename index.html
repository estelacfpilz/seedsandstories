Discover Bigodi, Uganda

We are based in Bigodi, a village rich in natural materials but which is suffering from the effects of environmental degradation due to human activities.
Seeds & Stories’ mission is to combat environmental degradation and contribute to Bigodi’s environmental regeneration through education and vocational training, helping the population build a circular, self-sufficient, community-based economy. We offers training on permaculture, regenerative agriculture, waste management, and water conservation. .

Discover our vocational training

Our artisans only use organic and sustainably harvested natural local fibres, including backcloth, palm leaf, millet straws, papyrus, raffia, colona maranta, and banana fibre. We only use earth-based dyes, a combination of food waste, plants, leaves, seeds, roots, barks and clay.
